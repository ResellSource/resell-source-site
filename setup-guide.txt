==============================
RESELL SOURCE — SETUP GUIDE
==============================

1️⃣ Unzip or open your folder
--------------------------------
Your folder structure should look like this:
resell-source/
│
├── index.html
├── products.html
├── thank-you-1.html ... thank-you-6.html
│
├── css/
│   └── style.css
├── js/
│   └── main.js
├── assets/
│   ├── logo.png
│   ├── bundle1.png
│   ├── bundle2.png
│   ├── northface.png
│   ├── geedup.png
│   ├── luxury.png
│   └── airpods.png
├── downloads/
│   ├── bundle1.pdf
│   ├── bundle2.pdf
│   ├── northface.pdf
│   ├── geedup.pdf
│   ├── luxury.pdf
│   └── airpods.pdf
└── setup-guide.txt

2️⃣ Add your product images
--------------------------------
In the /assets folder:
- Place your 6 Apple-style product images (PNG or JPG).
- Keep the file names exactly as listed above.

3️⃣ Add your PDF instruction files
--------------------------------
In the /downloads folder:
- Drop in each PDF that matches your thank-you pages:
  bundle1.pdf, bundle2.pdf, northface.pdf, geedup.pdf, luxury.pdf, airpods.pdf

4️⃣ Check your Stripe links
--------------------------------
